About Me

Alec Inthalangsy was born in Atlanta, Georgia in 1993. He grew up in the suburbs where he attended Roswell High School and later graduated from Sequoyah High School. He currently holds a bachelors degree in Economics from Georgia State University


He loves to read books, exercise and play sports. His favorite teams are the Atlanta Falcons and Georgia Bulldogs. He rarely misses a game.


On his spare time you can see him reading, exercising, coding or having a beer with his friends. He was been to 10 countries and plans to travel the world even more. His favorite city that he has visited was Florence, Italy.
